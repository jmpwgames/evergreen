#Evergreen

A procedurally generated queer romance kinetic novel in a single HTML file

Evergreen tells the story of two people finding each other and building love through 25 pages of intimate, literary prose. Each playthrough generates a unique narrative from over 1,250 hand-written sentences, adapting to your preferences for character identities, pronouns, romance routes (WLW/MLM/straight/queer), and optional trans-specific storylines.

Features:

Fully customizable characters with pronoun support (she/her, he/him, they/them, custom)
Dynamic route selection (lesbian, gay, straight, or queer narratives)
Procedural story generation that maintains narrative coherence
Save/load functionality with localStorage
Ambient music player with 8 tracks
Mobile-responsive design with dark forest aesthetic
~1,200 lines of code, entirely self-contained
